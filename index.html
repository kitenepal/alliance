About us

Today the world is running under the technology. Without technological knowledge, our daily activities are being difficult. The whole world is captured by technology. Whether it is most developed country or least developed country, the impact of technology is seen everywhere.

In Nepal, there is a great impact of technology. The youth people have their bright future in the field of technology because India and China are the technologically developed countries. To be a professional technological manpower, knowledge about it is must. Without knowledge, every field is blank and little knowledge is most dangerous. So, to gain complete knowledge about the technological field i.e., computer, networking devices, server handling and so on, a perfect training center should be chosen.
